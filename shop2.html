ARE ALLBIRDS WASHABLE?

Absolutely! Here's how it's done: Begin by taking out the insoles and laces, then use a towel or soft brush to sweep off any loose dirt. Place the shoes in the washing machine on the wool/delicates cycle (use cold water). We highly recommend putting the shoes in a linen bag or a pillowcase for extra protection. Always use a mild detergent and steer clear of bleach entirely. Then air dry for at least a day; do not tumble dry. (Learn more about caring for your Allbirds here.)
